About Me

I am a passionate BSc Physics student with a strong interest in technology and software development. My journey started with Python, where I built several text-based projects like contact management systems, task managers, and calculators. My academic background also includes simulating physics concepts such as straight-line fitting, Newton-Raphson methods, and projectile motion using Python.
I later expanded my skills to include front-end web development, where I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, creating mockups of popular platforms and interactive projects like a classic calculator with 3D effects. I also ventured into Android development, where I built apps like a counter and a WebView app with advanced navigation features.
Currently, I am focused on mastering Bootstrap for responsive web design and plan to dive into back-end technologies soon. With a mindset of continuous learning and a passion for solving problems, I aspire to create impactful digital solutions and grow as a versatile developer.
